Yujie Lu

Yujie Lu (卢雨洁) is a final year CS PhD at UC Santa Barbara, advised by William Wang at Natural Language Processing Group. She did research internship in Meta AI (FAIR), Microsoft Research (MSR), AWS AI. She obtained bachelor's degree from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University.

Research

Yujie's research focuses on vision language models (VLMs) and large language models (LLMs), with an emphasis on robust and efficient post-training (text, image, video), interpretable and faithful evaluation, task planning with LLM agents, and alignment with human preferences.
